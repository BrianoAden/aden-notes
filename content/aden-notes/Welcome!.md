This is the new, first note for my online notetaking journey. Here I will detail what I'd like to get out of using Obsidian as a notetaking app, and how I plan on doing it!

I want to be able to:
1. Have clear groups of content, or "[Maps of Content](Maps of Content)", as I've seen them referred to. 
2. Have it be easy to create a note on the fly.
	1. I want it to be clear whether it is a WIP, or a finished note
	2. I want there to be backlinks to a higher-level or related concept.
	3. Embedded in the note are links to other notes, to make navigation easy!
3. Ultimately, I want to post this online so that others can reference and learn alongside me.
