## Overview

The objective of the course is to:
- study the evolution of the architectures of advanced uniprocessor systems
- the influence of technology (hardware and sw) on architecture
- advanced pipelining techniques
- advanced memory system architecture including hierarchical cache memory
- RISC-V ISA and tools
- Opcode design: RISC 32-bit and compressed opcodes
- Techniques for exploiting instruction level parallelism, such as VLIW and vector machines
