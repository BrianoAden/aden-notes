 In this page, I will discuss the high-level function of Configurable Logic Blocks, or CLBs, as well as their low-level implementation.

## What are CLBs?
CLBs are the fundamental logic block of an FPGA. They are made up of two basic components: [[Flip-Flops]], or [[Lookup Tables (LUTs)]].
