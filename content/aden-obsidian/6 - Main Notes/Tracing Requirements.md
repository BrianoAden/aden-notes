There are a few good reasons to trace requirements within a model. 
1. We can trace requirements to an element that does work to verify said requirement
2. In the case of a FMEA:
	1. We can trace requirements to FMEA items to show what requirement might be affected in the event of a failure
	2. We can trace requirements to FMEA items for the sake of understanding what might be affected given a change to the traced requirement
	3. And also establishing general relationships between requirements and other model elements
